About MG Scientific Solutions

An independent scientific consulting firm founded by two PhDs with deep expertise across chemistry, advanced materials, quality systems, and life sciences — built across four continents.

Who We Are

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a trusted scientific and innovation partner that helps organizations transform ideas into successful products through scientific expertise, manufacturing support, quality excellence, and AI-enabled innovation.

We envision a future where startups, manufacturers, and innovators have access to world-class scientific guidance, practical solutions, and domestic manufacturing expertise, accelerating the development of products that improve lives and strengthen industry.

Our Mission

MG Scientific Solutions empowers organizations by providing scientific consulting, process development, manufacturing support, quality systems expertise, and AI-driven solutions.

We partner with companies across chemicals, ingredients, cosmetics, advanced materials, biotechnology, and life sciences to solve technical challenges, optimize processes, accelerate innovation, and enable successful product commercialization.

Through integrity, expertise, collaboration, and continuous innovation, we help our clients move from concept to manufacturing with confidence.

Our Founders

Who We Are

GA

Guillermo Ahumada, PhD

Co-Founder & Principal Scientific Consultant
Dual PhD in Chemistry — Université de Rennes 1 & Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso

Guillermo brings over a decade of deep scientific expertise spanning organic chemistry, process development, advanced materials, electrochemistry, and nanotechnology. He has conducted research at world-class institutions across four continents — including IBS (South Korea), KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden), and UT Austin — and currently serves as Director of R&D at Mironova Labs, where he leads the full development lifecycle from concept through commercial-scale manufacturing for specialty chemical and cosmetic ingredient clients.

His hands-on experience covers multi-step synthesis, scale-up from milligram to kilogram, CDMO management, QA/QC systems, DoE-driven process optimization, and analytical characterization (NMR, HPLC, GC-MS, and more). He combines classical scientific rigor with modern data science capabilities in Python and AI-driven approaches, enabling smarter, faster paths to product commercialization.

Organic Chemistry Process Development Scale-Up Electrochemistry CDMO Management QA/QC Advanced Materials AI & Data Science
MB

Magdalena Borkowska, PhD

Co-Founder & Head of Quality & Regulatory Affairs
PhD in Biological Sciences  |  15+ years across nanomedicine, cell & gene therapy, GMP quality systems, and biomedical research

Magdalena brings a rare combination of deep bench science and strategic quality leadership — built across four countries and three continents. She spent over five years as Senior Research Fellow at IBS in South Korea, developing pioneering nanotechnology-based cancer therapeutics using gold nanoparticles, before transitioning into GMP-regulated cell and gene therapy environments in the United States.

Currently serving as Director of Quality Partnerships at Minaris Advanced Therapies — a global CDMO leader in cell and gene therapy — she oversees quality governance, client partnerships, and GMP compliance across an international manufacturing network. Her expertise spans FDA cGMP, EU GMP, and ICH frameworks, with hands-on experience in technology transfers, audit readiness, CAPA management, regulatory inspections, and quality systems for advanced therapy products.

GMP Quality Systems FDA & EU GMP Cell & Gene Therapy Quality Partnerships Technology Transfer Audit & Inspection Readiness Nanomedicine Flow Cytometry
