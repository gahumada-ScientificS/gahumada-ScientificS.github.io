1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies help websites function properly, improve user experience, remember preferences, and collect information about how visitors use the website. Cookies may be set directly by our website or by third-party services we use.

2. Types of Cookies We May Use

Necessary Cookies

Required for the website to function properly — supporting basic functions such as page navigation, security, and form submission. These generally cannot be disabled because they are essential for the website to operate.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website — pages visited, time spent, browser type, and general traffic patterns. This information helps us improve website content and performance.

Functional Cookies

Allow the website to remember choices or preferences you make, providing a more personalized experience.

Third-Party Cookies

Our website may include links, embedded content, forms, or integrations from third-party providers. These third parties may place cookies on your device when you interact with their content or services. MG Scientific Solutions does not control third-party cookies — please review the privacy and cookie policies of those providers directly.

Third-party services used on or linked from our website may include, depending on current configuration: website hosting providers, Google Fonts, contact form tools (Formspree), and professional networking platforms such as LinkedIn.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, delete, or block cookies and set preferences for certain websites. If you choose to block or delete cookies, some parts of our website may not function properly.
