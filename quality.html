Our Certifications & Standards

The Entire Chain Is Quality-Governed

MG Scientific Solutions operates under an ISO 9001 quality system for management and data review. We define the test strategy, determine which experiments are scientifically relevant for your material and use case, and coordinate testing exclusively through US partner facilities that hold formal ISO 17025 accreditation.

We do not run the tests ourselves — and that is by design. The analysis is performed by an independent accredited laboratory. We then review the raw data, cross-verify the results against your supplier's CoA, and issue the final Qualification Report under MG Scientific Solutions as the independent third party. No conflict of interest. No ambiguity about who verified the lot.

Not every engagement requires a full qualification framework — and we understand that. We adapt to the scope, stage, and budget of each client. Whether you need a formal report or simply a trusted scientific opinion, we are here to help.

The Process

How a Material Qualification Works

From the first conversation to the final report — here is exactly what happens when you engage MG Scientific Solutions for an independent material qualification.

01

Scope & Test Strategy

We start by understanding your material, its intended use, your development stage, and what your buyer or regulator actually requires. We then define which analytical tests are scientifically relevant — not just a standard panel, but a strategy tailored to your situation.

02

MNDA Execution

Before any proprietary information changes hands, a Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement is signed. Your formulas, ingredients, and supplier data are protected from the start.

03

Sample Collection & Chain of Custody

We coordinate or directly manage sample collection from your facility or point of receipt. Every sample is logged, labeled, and tracked under a formal chain-of-custody protocol from this point forward.

04

US Laboratory Testing

Samples are routed exclusively to accredited US analytical laboratories holding ISO 17025 certification. Methods are selected to match the test strategy defined in Step 01 — HPLC, GC-MS, NMR, ICP-MS, FTIR, and others as required.

05

Data Review & CoA Cross-Verification

We receive the raw analytical data directly from the laboratory and review it internally — checking instrument outputs, integration, and methodology — before comparing results against your supplier's Certificate of Analysis.

06

Qualification Report & Technical Interpretation

We issue a formal US Qualification Report under MG Scientific Solutions, including a plain-language technical interpretation of the findings. If everything passes — you have your document. If something doesn't — we move to Step 07.

07

Technical Resolution (When Needed)

If a material fails or raises concerns, we don't stop at the report. We identify the root cause and define a practical path forward — whether that means additional testing, an impurity investigation, supplier communication, specification revision, purification strategy, reformulation, or identification of an alternative vendor.
