Who We Are

GA

Guillermo Ahumada, PhD

Co-Founder & Principal Scientific Consultant
Dual PhD in Chemistry — Université de Rennes 1 & Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso  |  H-Index: 12  |  33 peer-reviewed publications

Guillermo brings over a decade of deep scientific expertise spanning organic chemistry, process development, advanced materials, electrochemistry, and nanotechnology. He has conducted research at world-class institutions across four continents — including IBS (South Korea), KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden), and UT Austin — and currently serves as Director of R&D at Mironova Labs, where he leads the full development lifecycle from concept through commercial-scale manufacturing for specialty chemical and cosmetic ingredient clients.

His hands-on experience covers multi-step synthesis, scale-up from milligram to kilogram, CDMO management, QA/QC systems, DoE-driven process optimization, and analytical characterization (NMR, HPLC, GC-MS, and more). He combines classical scientific rigor with modern data science capabilities in Python and AI-driven approaches, enabling smarter, faster paths to product commercialization.

MB

Magdalena Borkowska, PhD

Co-Founder & Head of Quality & Regulatory Affairs
PhD in Biological Sciences  |  15+ years across nanomedicine, cell & gene therapy, GMP quality systems, and biomedical research

Magdalena brings a rare combination of deep bench science and strategic quality leadership — built across four countries and three continents. She spent over five years as Senior Research Fellow at IBS in South Korea, developing pioneering nanotechnology-based cancer therapeutics using gold nanoparticles, before transitioning into GMP-regulated cell and gene therapy environments in the United States.

Currently serving as Director of Quality Partnerships at Minaris Advanced Therapies — a global CDMO leader in cell and gene therapy — she oversees quality governance, client partnerships, and GMP compliance across an international manufacturing network. Her expertise spans FDA cGMP, EU GMP, and ICH frameworks, with hands-on experience in technology transfers, audit readiness, CAPA management, regulatory inspections, and quality systems for advanced therapy products. She bridges the gap between technical execution and strategic quality oversight with precision and clarity.
