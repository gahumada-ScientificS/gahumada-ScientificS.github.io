Engagement 01

A Startup Needs a Scientific Team

Biotechnology
The Challenge

A biotechnology startup has secured initial funding and identified a promising active ingredient but lacks internal scientific leadership to move forward.

Our Role

We developed a technical roadmap, identified analytical testing partners, evaluated manufacturing options, and coordinated all development activities from concept to commercialization.

The Outcome

The client gained immediate access to experienced scientific leadership without the cost of building a full internal R&D department.

Engagement 02

Entering the U.S. Market

Specialty Chemicals
The Challenge

An international specialty chemical manufacturer seeks to expand into the U.S. but lacks local technical representation and industry connections.

Our Role

We acted as the company's scientific liaison — identifying potential customers, analytical laboratories, and manufacturing partners, while supporting technical discussions with prospective clients.

The Outcome

The client accelerated market entry while reducing the risks associated with navigating an unfamiliar business environment.

Engagement 03

Selecting the Right Manufacturing Partner

Personal Care
The Challenge

A personal care company preparing to scale production of a new product receives multiple proposals from contract manufacturers and lacks the expertise to evaluate them.

Our Role

We reviewed technical capabilities, evaluated manufacturing strategies, participated in technical meetings, and helped select the most appropriate partner based on quality, scalability, and project requirements.

The Outcome

The client avoided costly delays and gained confidence that the selected manufacturing partner could support long-term growth.
