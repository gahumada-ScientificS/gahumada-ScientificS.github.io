US Material Qualification & Verification

Imported Materials.
Independently Verified. US-Certified.

Many companies source raw materials from suppliers in Asia or other international markets — materials that arrive with a Certificate of Analysis (CoA) from a foreign laboratory. US buyers increasingly need more than that.

We serve as your independent US-based scientific liaison — collecting samples, routing them to accredited American analytical laboratories, and issuing a formal US Qualification Report that independently confirms identity, purity, and compliance with your specifications. One document. Full credibility.

  • —Sample collection & chain-of-custody management
  • —Routing to accredited US analytical labs (HPLC, GC-MS, NMR, ICP-MS & more)
  • —CoA cross-verification & discrepancy analysis
  • —Formal US Qualification Report issued under MG SS
  • —Ideal for cosmetic, specialty chemical & pharma raw materials

Engagement 04

Verifying an Imported Raw Material for the US Market

Supply Chain
The Challenge

A US-based formulator sources a specialty ingredient from an Asian supplier. The material arrives with a foreign CoA, but their customer requires independent US-laboratory confirmation before it can be used in production.

Our Role

We coordinated sample collection, submitted the material to an accredited US analytical laboratory, cross-verified the results against the supplier's CoA, and issued a formal US Qualification Report under MG Scientific Solutions.

The Outcome

The client was able to proceed with production on schedule, with a single document that satisfied their customer's requirements and provided full traceability of the qualification process.
