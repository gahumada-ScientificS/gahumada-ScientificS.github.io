Privacy Policy

MG Scientific Solutions LLC  ·  Effective Date: June 10, 2026

Protecting your private information is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how MG Scientific Solutions LLC ("MG Scientific Solutions," "MGSS," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, protects, and discloses information when you visit our website or contact us regarding our scientific, technical, consulting, manufacturing-support, analytical, formulation, quality, or related services.

By using our website or submitting information to us, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We May Collect

MG Scientific Solutions may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • First and last name
  • Company name
  • Job title or role
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business address
  • Information included in messages, forms, project inquiries, requests for proposals, or communications with us

We do not collect personal information from you unless you voluntarily provide it to us, such as by contacting us through our website, email, phone, LinkedIn, or other business communication channels.

2. Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, access times, referring website addresses, pages viewed, and general website usage data. This information is used to operate and improve our website and services.

3. Use of Your Information

MG Scientific Solutions may use the information we collect to respond to inquiries, communicate regarding requested services, prepare proposals or quotations, provide scientific and technical services, improve our website and client experience, send business updates, maintain internal records, and comply with legal obligations. We do not use your information for purposes unrelated to our business relationship without your consent where required.

4. Sharing of Information with Third Parties

MG Scientific Solutions does not sell, rent, or lease customer lists or personal information to third parties. We may share information only when necessary with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business — such as website hosting providers, email providers, IT support, and accounting or legal advisors. These providers are expected to protect your information and use it only to provide services to us.

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of MG Scientific Solutions, our clients, or the public.

5. Confidential Business and Technical Information

Because MG Scientific Solutions provides scientific and technical consulting services, clients or prospective clients may submit confidential business, scientific, or technical information. MG Scientific Solutions treats such information with care. However, unless a separate written confidentiality agreement is in place, information submitted through the website or general email may not automatically create a formal confidential relationship. For sensitive technical projects, we recommend executing a written confidentiality agreement before sharing proprietary information.

6. Use of Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website functionality and understand how visitors interact with our site. You may manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. For full details, please review our Cookie Notice.

7. Email Communications

MG Scientific Solutions may contact you by email regarding your inquiry, requested services, project discussions, proposals, or business updates. You may request that we stop sending non-essential communications by contacting us at the email address below.

8. Data Security

MG Scientific Solutions takes reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption to help protect transmitted information. However, no Internet transmission or electronic storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

9. Data Retention

MG Scientific Solutions retains personal and business information only for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including legal obligations, accounting requirements, dispute resolution, and legitimate business needs.

10. Your Privacy Choices

You may contact us to request that we confirm, correct, or delete personal information about you, or stop sending non-essential communications. We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests. Note that we may retain information necessary to complete a transaction, comply with legal obligations, or maintain required business records.

11. Children's Privacy

MG Scientific Solutions does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our website and services are intended for business and professional audiences.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. MG Scientific Solutions is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

MG Scientific Solutions reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acknowledgment of the updated Privacy Policy.

14. Accessibility

MG Scientific Solutions is committed to making this website accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We strive to ensure that our digital content is usable by people employing assistive technologies such as screen readers, magnifiers, or voice recognition software. If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this site, please contact us at info@mgscientificsolutions.com and we will do our best to assist you and address any accessibility concerns promptly.
