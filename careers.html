Who We Look For

Profiles We Are Always Interested In

We look for people who combine deep technical knowledge with the ability to communicate clearly, think independently, and deliver practical results for real clients.

Scientific Consulting

Chemists & Materials Scientists

PhD or MSc in chemistry, chemical engineering, materials science, or related fields. Experience in process development, synthesis, scale-up, formulation, or analytical characterization. Comfortable working across multiple projects and industries.

Quality & Compliance

Quality & Regulatory Professionals

Background in GMP quality systems, regulatory affairs, or compliance for pharmaceutical, biotech, cosmetic, or specialty chemical industries. Experience with ISO standards, audits, SOPs, or CAPA management is highly valued.

Scientific Business Development

Scientists with a Commercial Edge

Scientists who enjoy working with clients, identifying new opportunities, and translating technical capabilities into business value. Comfortable leading client conversations, preparing proposals, and building long-term relationships.

Life Sciences

Biotech & Life Sciences Specialists

Experience in cell and gene therapy, biologics, nanomedicine, or advanced therapy products. Familiarity with GMP manufacturing environments, technology transfer, or quality systems for regulated biological products.

Analytical Sciences

Analytical Chemists

Hands-on experience with HPLC, GC-MS, NMR, ICP-MS, FTIR, or other analytical techniques. Ability to design test strategies, interpret raw data, and communicate findings clearly to non-specialist audiences.

AI & Data Science

Scientists with AI & Data Skills

Scientists who also work in Python, data analysis, machine learning, or AI tool development. Interest in applying AI to real scientific workflows — literature review, documentation, process optimization, or laboratory productivity.
